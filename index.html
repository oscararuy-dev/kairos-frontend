What Kairos does

Everything your eval workflow needs

Built specifically for school psychologists who use the CHC framework and need defensible, time-efficient results.

🧠
AI Score Extraction
Upload score reports as PDFs or photos. Kairos extracts all subtests and composites automatically using Claude vision — no more manual transcription.
⚡
PSW / XBA Analysis
Runs the full Aron & Breaux PSW and cross-battery analysis engine. Identifies strengths, weaknesses, and convergent evidence across 10+ CHC domains.
📋
Dyslexia Pattern Detection
Identifies IDA, DDH, and CDE dyslexia profiles automatically. Flags convergent evidence for SLD eligibility using current best practice criteria.
🔍
Multi-Instrument Support
Works with WJ-IV, WISC-V, KABC-II, CTOPP-2, WIAT-4, BASC-3, and more. Handles cross-battery comparisons with appropriate shared-ability controls.
📊
Visual Report Output
Generates clear CHC domain summary figures and interpretive tables ready to paste directly into your evaluation report.
🔒
FERPA-Conscious Design
No student names or identifying data required. Processing happens securely without storing raw score data — designed with school privacy requirements in mind.
